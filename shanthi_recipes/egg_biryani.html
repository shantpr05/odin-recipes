Egg Biryani

Descrition

Egg Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, boiled eggs, and a blend of spices. It's a delicious and easy-to-make dish that can be enjoyed as a main course. The eggs are cooked in a rich and spicy gravy, and then layered with rice to create a mouthwatering biryani that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4-6 Eggs, boiled and peeled
  • 1 large Onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup Ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Bay leaf)
  • 2.5 cups Water

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the Basmati rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee in a large pot and add whole spices. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste, followed by the chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add the yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook the mixture for 10 minutes on low heat.
  6. Add the boiled eggs to the mixture and gently coat them with the spices. Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add the drained rice and water to the pot. Mix gently.
  8. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 20 minutes, or until rice is done.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita or salad.
